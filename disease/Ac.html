In adults, the majority of food allergies are triggered by certain proteins in:


1)Shellfish, such as shrimp, lobster and crab

2)Peanuts

3)Tree nuts, such as walnuts and pecans

4)Fish

In children, food allergies are commonly triggered by proteins in:


1)Peanuts

2)Tree nuts

3)Eggs

4)Cow's milk

5)Wheat

6)Soy
