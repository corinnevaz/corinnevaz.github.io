A cough can be caused by several conditions, both temporary and permanent.

1) Clearing the throat

2) Viruses and bacteria

3) Smoking

4) Asthma

5) Medicines

6) Damage to the vocal cords

7) Postnasal drip

8) Bacterial infections such as pneumonia, whooping cough, and croup

9) Serious conditions such as pulmonary embolism and heart failure
