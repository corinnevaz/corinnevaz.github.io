What is Aspitrate?

Capsule widens blood vessels allowing for an increase of blood flow to the heart. It is also used to avoid an angina attack. Aspitrate 30 Mg/150 Mg Capsule is sold under different tade names in different countries.

Side Effects

Please inform your doctor if you experience any adverse reaction to the medication.

  • Bluish discoloration of lips, fingernails, palm, or hands
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Low blood pressure
  • Dizziness and fainting
  • Headache
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Chest pain
  • Difficulty in breathing
  • Difficulty in swallowing
  • Hives and redness of skin
  • Burning, numbness, tingling in the arms and feet
  • Agitation and anxiety
  • Sleeplessnes
