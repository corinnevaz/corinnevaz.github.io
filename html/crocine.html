What is Crocine?

Crocin Advance Tablet is used to temporarily relieve fever and mild to moderate pain such as muscle ache, headache, toothache, arthritis, and backache. This medicine should be used with caution in patients with liver diseases due to the increased risk of severe adverse effects.

Substitutes

Dolo 650 Tablet
Combiflam Tablet
Parafast 500 MG Tablet
Temfix 500 MG Tablet

What is it used for?

  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Muscle Pain
  • Menstrual Cramps
  • Post Immunization Pyrexia
  • Arthritis

What are the Side-Effects?

  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Fever
  • Allergic skin reaction
  • Gastric / Mouth Ulcer
  • Anemia(RARE)
  • Fatigue
  • Unusual bleeding or bruising
