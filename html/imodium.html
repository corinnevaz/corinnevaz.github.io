What is Imodium?

Imodium 2 MG Capsule is a very effective anti-diarrhea medication. It is used to relieve the symptoms of diarrhea that starts suddenly and lasts for a few days. It is also used to treat long-term diarrhea associated with other diseases.

What is it used for?

What are the Side-Effects?

  • Bloating
  • Constipation(SEVERE)(RARE)
  • Loss of appetite
  • Severe stomach ache
  • Dizziness
  • Dry mouth
  • Allergic skin reaction
