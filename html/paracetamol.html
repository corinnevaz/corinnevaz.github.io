What is Paracetamol ?

Paracetamol 650 MG Tablet is used to temporarily relieve fever and mild to moderate pain such as muscle ache, headache, toothache, arthritis, and backache. This medicine should be used with caution in patients with liver diseases due to the increased risk of severe adverse effects.

What are the Side-Effects?

  • Allergic skin reaction
  • Gastric / Mouth Ulcer
  • Anemia(RARE)
  • Fatigue
  • Unusual bleeding or bruising
